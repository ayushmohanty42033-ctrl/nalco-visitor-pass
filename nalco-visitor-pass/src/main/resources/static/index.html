Leading the Aluminium Revolution

National Aluminium Company Limited (NALCO) is a Navratna CPSE under the Ministry of Mines, Government of India. Operating massive integrated mining, alumina, power, and smelter operations, security of assets, infrastructure, and human resources remains our highest institutional priority.

The digital Visitor Pass Management System replaces paper logbooks with end-to-end encrypted identification check-ins, visitor pre-screening, blacklist matches, and instant host notification alerts.

Mandatory Security Policy

Access to NALCO industrial zones is subject to the following rules:

  • Valid Gov. identity card (Aadhaar, PAN, License, or Passport) must match registration.
  • Pre-registered OTP verifications for mobile and email contact details are mandatory.
  • Random physical safety briefing and gear verification at security checkpoints.

How to Acquire Your Entry Pass

1

1. Register Account

Create a secure account with your contact numbers, verify via live OTP, and upload your photo along with Government identity documentation.

2

2. Apply for Visit

Search host employees, select departments, define date/times, specify your purpose, and submit for security approval clearance.

3

3. Download QR Pass

Once approved, print or download your pass. Security guards will verify the QR code at NALCO check gates to activate entry.
